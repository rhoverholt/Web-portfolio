About Me

Are you ready for a different kind of bio?

Ivy League degrees in Computer Science and Mathematics launched my IT career back in 1991. After several years with a small consulting firm based in Princeton, NJ I transitioned into a management role at Norfolk Southern Railroad. My career ran the full cycle of project development: from a twinkle in someone's eye; to a well-defined business case; to research time on the job developing requirements; to core software design, development, testing; and even into training and implementation and eventual software maintenance.

In 2005 personal tragedy struck and I chose to retire from a wonderful IT career and stay home to raise my son. At the youthful age of 53, with my son off to college, I am returning to the IT field that I love targeting a more specifically technical role.
